THE ANT ADVENTURE

Information

This demo reel showcases my animation work through a carefully crafted scene featuring an ant’s journey. The animation follows the ant as it walks naturally into the frame, displaying precise, hand-keyed motion. It navigates its environment with curiosity, encountering a towering blade of grass and beginning its ascent with detailed limb coordination and weight distribution. However, gravity soon takes over, causing the ant to lose its grip and fall—an unexpected yet organic moment that adds realism and character. The sequence concludes with the project title appearing, seamlessly integrating with the animation. This reel highlights my focus on expressive character movement, physics-based animation, and environmental interaction, demonstrating my skills in 3D animation and storytelling.
