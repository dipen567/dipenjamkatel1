The Ant Adventure

Information

It’s a story that begins in an ant colony where group of ants starts a journey in search of bigger and better food. This story takes place in a wide-open landscape so there will be varieties of plants all around. All the assets will have small animations to give life to them. Since, ant would be the main character for this animation there won't be varieties of camera angles. As the group of ants advance towards the story, they catch the smell of a fruit, and they decide to go search for the food. When they approach near to the fruit they encounter antagonist of the story Lizard. Here, in this animation the back story of the lizard isn’t focused so the encounter would be all sudden. Moving to the last part of the story, there would be a battle between ants and lizard for the food. Since ants are very good for their teamwork, they end up making lizard their food too. And the story ends where ants carry their food and lizard to their colony.
